Some Facts about Spain

Spain is a country located in Southwestern Europe, with a population of approximately 47 million people. It is known for its rich history, diverse culture, and stunning architecture. The official language is Spanish, and the country also has several regional languages such a Catalan, Basque, and Galician. Spain is a popular tourist destination, with attractions such as the beaches of the Costa del Sol, the architecture of Barcelona, and the historic cities of Madrid and Seville. The country's economy is based on industries such as tourism, agriculture, and manufacturing. Spain is also a major producer of wine and olive oil. The country has a parliamentary system of government, with the monarch serving as a ceremonial head of state.

Popular Cities in Spain


Places to Visit in Madrid


Museo Nacional Centro de Arte Reina Sofia

This museum is Spain's national museum that contains 20th century Spanish artwork from artists such as Pablo Picasso and Salvador Dali.

San Miguel Market

This local food market contains dishes from all parts of the country that can be sampled.


Places to Visit in Barcelona


Mercado de La Boqueria

This public food market offers local and international food products and is known as the heart of Barcelona.

Park Guell

This park contains gardens and architectural structures by Catalan architect Antoni Gaudi.
