Some Facts about Country

Malaysia is a country located in Southeast Asia, known for its diverse cultures, beautiful landscapes, and vibrant cities. It is composed of two main regions, Peninsular Malaysia and East Malaysia, and has a population of over 32 million people. The country's official language is Bahasa Malaysia, but English, Chinese, and Tamil are also commonly spoken. Malaysia's economy is one of the most developed in the region, driven by exports of natural resources such as oil, gas, and palm oil, as well as manufacturing and tourism. The country is also known for its delicious cuisine, which blends Malay, Chinese, Indian, and other influences. Malaysia's tropical rainforests are home to several endangered species including malayan tigers, sumatran rhinoceroses, and pygmy elephants.
