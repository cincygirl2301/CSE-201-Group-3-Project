Some Facts about Malaysia

Malaysia is a country located in Southeast Asia, known for its diverse cultures, beautiful landscapes, and vibrant cities. It is composed of two main regions, Peninsular Malaysia and East Malaysia, and has a population of over 32 million people. The country's official language is Bahasa Malaysia, but English, Chinese, and Tamil are also commonly spoken. Malaysia's economy is one of the most developed in the region, driven by exports of natural resources such as oil, gas, and palm oil, as well as manufacturing and tourism. The country is also known for its delicious cuisine, which blends Malay, Chinese, Indian, and other influences. Malaysia's tropical rainforests are home to several endangered species including malayan tigers, sumatran rhinoceroses, and pygmy elephants.

Popular Cities in Malaysia


Places to Visit in Kuala Lumpur


Petronas Towers

This pair of skyscrapers are the tallest twin towers in the world with 88 floors.

Kuala Lumpur Bird Park

This park has over 200 species of birds and activites like bird feedings and bird shows. This park also includes a garden, the National Monument, and the Kuala Lumpur Butterfly Park.


Places to Visit in Langkawi


Telaga Tujuh Waterfall

The name Telaga Tujuh means seven Wells Waterfalls, which references the seven connecting natural pools that are fed by seven separate Waterfalls.

Langkawi SkyBridge Cable Car

A fifteen minute cable car ride that is 1.4 miles long gives the riders a view of the forest, the Telaga Tujuh waterfall, and the sea.

Kilim Geoforest Park

This 100 square kilometer nature reserve was the first geopark in Southeast Asia. Tours are given by boat on the Kilim River.


Places to Visit in Kota Kinabalu


River Cruise on the Klias River

This cruise gives a tour of the Klias wetlands where you can see proboscis monkeys, otters, crocodiles, and other wetland creatures.

Kota Kinabalu City Mosque

The official opening of this mosque followed the proclamation of the city status of Kota Kinabalu in 2000, Visitors can be given tours and can go paddle boat riding.
