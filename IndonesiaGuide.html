Some Facts About Indonesia

Indonesia is the largest country in Southeast Asia. The maximum dimensions from east to west is 3,200 miles long and 1,100 miles long from north to south with a total area of oer 1 million square kilometers. Indonesia has a population of over 278 million people and is known for its tropical beaches and religious temples.

Places to Visit in Bali


Waterbom Bali

This water park has rides, restaurants, and swim-up bars.

Ubud Monkey Forest

This sanctuary is home to three Hindu temples, 155 species of trees, and 1,260 Balinese long-tailed macaque monkeys.

Tirta Empul

This sacred water temple is famous for its holy water spring that Balinese Hindus go to for ritual purification.

Nyang Nyang Beach

This beach on the southern coast of Bali is known for its white sand.
