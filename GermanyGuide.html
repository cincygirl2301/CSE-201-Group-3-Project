Some Facts About Germany

Germany is a country that is mostly known for its beer. In this country you can find about 1,500 breweries where over 7,000 types of beer can be found. Germany has a population of about 85 million people. This country's total area is 357,592 square kilometers and about one-third of it is woodlands and forests.

Popular Cities in Germany


Places to Visit in Munich


English Garden

This is the largest park in Munich, located in the center of the city. Its main attractions are the Monopteros, the Chinese Tower, and the Japanese Tower.

Asam Church

This church was built by the Asam brothers as their private church and is now one of the most important buildings of the German Late Baroque.

Hellabrunn Zoo

This zoo features a high ratio of enclosures that are cageless, relying on moats to keep animals in place. This was the first zoo to be organized by geographical aspects rather than species.


Places to Visit in Berlin


Berlin Wall Memorial

A former board of the Berlin Wall that once separated the city of berlin now serves as a window of remembrance for visitors to see.

Brandenburg Gate

This neoclassic monument was built in the 18th century and is known as a symbol of the history of Germany and Europe as well as European unity.


Places to Visit in Cologne


Cologne Cathedral

This cathedral is the largest gothic church in northern Europe at 515 feet tall.

Belgian Quarter

This area is located in the inner city district of Cologne features cuisine and is known as a meeting place for the creative arts.

Ludwig Museum

This museum features modern art works from pop art, surrealism, and abstract. It also has one of the largest Picasso collections in Europe, as well as works by Andy Worhol and Roy Lichenstein.
