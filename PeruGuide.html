Some Facts about Peru

Peru is a country located in South America, with a population of approximately 33 million people. The country is home to diverse landscapes, including the Andes Mountains, the Amazon Rainforest, and the Pacific coast. The official languages are Spanish, Quechua, and Aymara. Peru is known for its rich history, with many ancient civilizations leaving behind impressive archaeological sites, such as Machu Picchu and Chan Chan. The country's cuisine is also renowned, with dishes such as ceviche and lmo saltado gaining popularity worldwide. The economy is primarily based on agriculture, mining, and tourism. Peru is one of the world's largest producers of gold, silver, and copper.
