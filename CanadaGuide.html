Some Facts About Canada

Canada is the second largest country in the world with a total area of over 9 million square kilometers. This country takes up two-fifths of the North American continent, but even with its large size it is one of the most sparsely populated countries in the world. Canada's official language is French and English and its population is over 37 million people.

Places to Visit in Banff


Cave and Basin National Historic Site

This site is located in the Canadian Rocky Mountains at the thermal mineral springs.

Lake Minnewanka

This lake was known to the First Nations people as Minn-waki or “lake of the spirits” and can be explored by boat or on foot.


Places to Visit in Montreal


Notre-Dame Basilica of Montreal

This was the first Gothic revival style church to be built in Canada.

Biodome

This facility allows you to walk through replicas of different ecosystems found in North and South America.


Places to Visit in Vancouver


Vancouver Aquarium

The Vancouver Aquarium was the first public aquarium to open in Canada.

Capilano Suspension Bridge Park

This bridge crosses 230 feet above the Capilano River and is 460 feet long.
