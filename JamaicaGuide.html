Some Facts about Country

Jamaica is and island country located in the Caribbean Sea, south of Cuba. It has a population of approximately 2.7 million people (as of 2023) and its capital is Kingston. The official language is English, with its currency being the Jamaican dollar (JMD). Jamaica is known for its vibrant culture, including raggae music and its famous cuisine. The country has a diverse geography, with lush rainforests, beautiful beaches, and the Blue Mountains, which are home to the world famous Blue Mountain coffee. Jamaica is also known for its athletic prowess, having produced world-renowned athletes like Usain Bolt and a rich history of track and field success.

Places to Visit in Montego Bay


Doctor's Cave Beach

This beach features multiple restaurants and is part of the Montego Bay Marine Park, so you can go snorkeling among the coral reefs and their marine life.

Bamboo Rafting in the Martha Brae River

Ride on 30 feet long bamboo rafts through the Martha Brae River and learn about “The Legend of Martha Brae”.

Dunn's River Falls

This waterfall is 180 feet high and is terraced like giant natural stairs that can be climbed.
