Popular Cities in Morocco


Places to Visit in Marrakech


Koutoubia

This building is the largest Mosque in Morocco being 253 feet tall and is considered a classic example of Moroccan Mosque architecture.

House of Photography in Marrakech

This place features about 10,000 documents and vintage photographs from 1870 to 1960, with pictures of Moroccan landscapes, architecural complexes, and portraits.


Places to Visit in Essaouira


Azur Art and Spa

This luxurious spa and wellness center offers a wide range of treatments, including massages, facials, and hammam rituals, in a tranquil serence environment. The center also houses a contemporary art gallery featuring works by local and international artists.
