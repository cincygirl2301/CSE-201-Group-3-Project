Some Facts About Brazil

Brazil is the fifth largest country in the world with a total area of over 8 million square kilometers. This country shares a boarder with every country in South America execpt Ecuador and Chile. Brazil's official language is Portuguese and has a population of approximately 216 million people (as of 2023).

Places to Visit in Rio de Janeiro


Christ The Redeemer Statue

This giant statue of Jesus Christ stands on a 26 feet tall pedestal and is 98 feet tall and 92 feet long with its arms stretched.

Copacabana

Located in the southern part of the city, this beach is 2.5 miles long and is one of the most famous beaches in the world.

Rio Carnival 2023

Carnival is a festival held every year before Lent and is one of the biggest carnivals in the world.
