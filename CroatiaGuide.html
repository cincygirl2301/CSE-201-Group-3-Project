Places to Visit in Zagreb


Museum of Broken Relationships

This museum contains a collection of donated personal items devoted to failed relationships.

Cathedral of Zagreb

This 12th century Neo Gothic cathedral is the second largest building in Croatia.

Botanical Garden of the Faculty of Science

This facility is home to over 10,000 species of plants with 1,800 of them being exotic ones.


Places to Visit in Dubrovnik


Dubrovnik Cable Car

Takes passengers 778 meters to a plateau with a view of the city and restaurant.

The Cathedral of the Assumption of the Virgin Mary

This cathedral church was built during the years 1642 and 1646 in the Baroque style.

Lovrijenac

This historical fortress is a theater and was built on top of a 37 feet high cliff.
